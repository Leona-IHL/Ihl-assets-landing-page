About Us

IHL Asset Management is a wholly owned subsidiary of IBILE Holdings Limited. IHLA acts as fund managers to Government institutions, specialized funds, partner organizations, multinationals in Nigeria, and high-net-worth individuals, rendering services including portfolio management, scheme administration, and financial advisory.

Our Clients

General Investing Public

We’re dedicated to offering smart and tailored wealth management solutions for everyone looking to grow their investment. Drawing on our deep industry experience, we aim to exceed standard return expectations by providing access to top-rated investment solutions.

Sub-nationals and Affiliates

We align financial strategies with broader societal and economic benefits. Through strategic asset management, we help institutions achieve their investment goals by allocating resources effectively to support state initiatives such as infrastructure, education, public services, and community projects. The public’s investment decisions are driven with sound economic and long-term sustainable investment in mind.

Institutional Investors And Partners

We collaborate with institutional investors and partners to align investment strategies with broader economic opportunities and return. By employing strategic asset management, we allocate investments to opportunities that align with organizational objectives while also allowing for a positive social impact.

High net-worth Individuals

We work with high net-worth individuals to achieve their investment objectives. Our goal is to make sophisticated wealth and investment products more accessible, with simpler terms. Additionally, we provide alternative investment opportunities, leveraging our ownership to deliver solid returns in this area as well.
